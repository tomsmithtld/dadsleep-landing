Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 February 2026

What DadSleep collects

DadSleep stores your baby's sleep logs (times, durations, notes) locally on your device. We do not send this data to any server.

If you sign up for the waitlist on our website, we collect your email address. That's it.

Data storage

All sleep data, tips you've saved, and your preferences are stored on your device only. We cannot access it. If you delete the app, the data is gone.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (like which features are used most) to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.

Third parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Your baby's sleep data stays on your phone.

Waitlist emails

If you join the waitlist, we use your email only to notify you when DadSleep launches. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email.

Children

DadSleep is a tool for parents. The app tracks baby sleep patterns but does not collect data from or about children for advertising or profiling purposes.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with a new date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email tom@33c.dev.
